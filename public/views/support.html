ACLO Lustrumloop Support

How do I register?

Using the email address you provided to us during sign up, register with a safe password. On completion, it will take you back to the login screen.

How do I start?

In the "Match" tab, press start. Make sure not to lock your screen.

Where do I see other teams speed?

In the "Standings tab!" tab.

How is the winner determined?

The winner is based on average team speed.
